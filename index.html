Strategic Recommendations

Based on the comprehensive analysis, the following are actionable strategies designed to address e.l.f.'s key business objectives: reducing customer acquisition costs, enhancing brand perception, and expanding market share. These recommendations are tailored to leverage strengths and mitigate weaknesses.

Reduce CAC

Launch a user-generated content (UGC) challenge focused on product "dupes" to drive organic reach and reduce paid ad spend. Track #elfdupechallenge.
Increase incentive from 10% to 15% for both referrer and new customer in the "Beauty Squad" loyalty program to boost word-of-mouth acquisition.
Create targeted blog content and product pages that rank for high-intent keywords like "[Luxury Brand] alternative" or "best affordable [product type]".

Enhance Brand Perception

Launch a marketing campaign highlighting the high-quality ingredients (e.g., niacinamide, hyaluronic acid) and formulation science behind the affordable price point.
Introduce a pilot program for refillable packaging on a hero product like the Power Grip Primer to appeal to eco-conscious consumers and generate positive PR.
Partner with a respected, science-focused skincare influencer (e.g., a dermatologist on YouTube) to validate product efficacy and reach a more discerning audience.

Expand Market Share

Develop a new product line of skincare-makeup hybrids (e.g., tinted serums, SPF-infused primers) to capture the demand for multi-functional products.
Create a marketing campaign for consumers 40+ focusing on simple, effective, affordable routines, using age-appropriate influencers and platforms like Facebook and Instagram.
Prioritize expansion into high-growth markets like Southeast Asia and Latin America through localized e-commerce sites and partnerships with regional distributors.
